ABOUT US

Vidyalankar Institute of Technology was started in the year 1999 with a mission to provide an educational environment where students can reach their full potential in their chosen discipline and as individual persons. The Institute is Accredited A+ by NAAC , and is affiliated to Mumbai University and approved by AICTE.
Department of Information Technology , Vidyalankar Institute of Technology, Mumbai is conducting an Online Faculty Development Program on "Advanced Technology in IT" from 9th May 2020 – 12th May 2020.
The objective of this FDP is to propagate the key skills required to be accustomed to latest trending technologies in IT like Deep Learning, Block Chain, Cloud Computing, Advanced HTML for faculty across various domains.
